About Us

Drona Electronics is a trusted manufacturer of high-quality electronic parts and consumer products such as night lamps, spiritual sound devices, and home utility electronics. With a focus on innovation, affordability, and performance, we are committed to delivering products that blend functionality with thoughtful design.

Every product at Drona Electronics is carefully crafted and tested to meet rigorous quality standards. Whether you're sourcing components or looking for ready-to-use solutions, we provide reliable and durable electronics tailored to modern needs.

Located in India, we proudly serve both retail and wholesale clients across the country. With a growing product range and customer-first approach, we aim to be your go-to partner for all your electronic manufacturing needs.
